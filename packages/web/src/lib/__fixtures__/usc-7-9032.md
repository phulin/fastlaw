**(d)** **Seed cotton**

> > **(1)** **In general** 

> > For purposes of section 9016 (b)(2) of this title and paragraphs (1)(B)(ii) and (2)(A)(ii)(II) of [section 9017(b) of this title](/statutes/section/7/9017), the loan rate for seed cotton shall be deemed to be equal to $0.25 per pound.

> > **(2)** **Effect** 

> > Nothing in this subsection authorizes any nonrecourse marketing assistance loan under this subchapter for seed cotton.

Sec. 20-86aa. Doula advisory committee. Duties. Membership. (a) As used in this section:

(1) "Certified doula" means a doula that is certified by the Department of Public Health; and

(2) "Doula" means a trained, nonmedical professional who provides physical, emotional and informational support.

(b) The Commissioner of Public Health shall establish a Doula Advisory Committee within the Department of Public Health.

(P.A. 23-147, S. 13.)

History: P.A. 23-147 effective July 1, 2023.

Sec. 20-86bb. Doulas. Certification. Use of title. (a) As used in this section, (1) "certified doula" means a doula who is certified by the Department of Public Health.

(b) The Doula Advisory Committee shall advise the Commissioner on matters relating to doula services.

(P.A. 23-147, S. 14.)

History: P.A. 23-147 effective July 1, 2023.

Cited in various cases for doula certification standards.
